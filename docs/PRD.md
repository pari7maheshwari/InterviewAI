# Product Overview

InterviewAI is a personalized interview preparation platform designed to help college students, fresh graduates, developers, and job seekers prepare for technical interviews based on their individual background and career goals.

Unlike traditional interview preparation platforms that provide generic question banks, InterviewAI personalizes the interview experience using the user's resume, target role, and optionally the target company and job description. It conducts realistic AI-powered mock interviews with dynamic follow-up questions and provides actionable feedback to help users improve over time.

The primary objective of InterviewAI is to reduce the time users spend figuring out what to study while increasing their confidence through realistic, role-specific interview practice.

## Onboarding Philosophy

InterviewAI is designed to guide users through a personalized interview preparation journey rather than presenting a generic AI chatbot.

New users should first understand the platform's value through an interactive onboarding experience before being asked to create an account. The onboarding process should collect only the information necessary to immediately personalize the user's experience.

## User Goals

InterviewAI is designed around the user's objective rather than their demographic.

Instead of treating users differently based solely on whether they are students, fresh graduates, or working professionals, the platform personalizes the experience according to what they are trying to achieve.

Primary user goals include:

- Secure an internship
- Prepare for campus placements
- Prepare for a specific company
- Switch to a new role
- Improve interview confidence

These goals influence interview difficulty, roadmap generation, feedback, and follow-up questions.
